Buying a creator's token on Matiz takes about **5 minutes** and requires no
crypto knowledge whatsoever: just your Google account and a card. This is the
full visual guide, from clicking the link to seeing your portfolio.

**12 steps · ~5 min start to finish · network fee ~$0.01 · on-chain on Solana.**

## 01 · Connect — create your account & wallet

### 1. You've been invited to believe in someone

![Open the token link and tap Connect](/blog/como-comprar-tu-token/step-01.jpg)

Open a creator's token link and tap **Connect**. No crypto knowledge needed.

**→ Your action:** tap «Connect».

### 2. Choose how to sign in

![Choose Google or email, or connect your own wallet](/blog/como-comprar-tu-token/step-02.jpg)

Two paths: **Google or email** (we create the wallet for you) or connect your
own wallet.

**→ Your action:** choose «Google or email».

### 3. Log in or sign up

![Continue with one click using Google](/blog/como-comprar-tu-token/step-03.jpg)

Continue with one click using **Google**. Privy secures it: you never see seed
phrases.

**→ Your action:** continue with Google.

### 4. Connected with Google

![Successfully connected with Google](/blog/como-comprar-tu-token/step-04.jpg)

«Successfully connected with Google.» Your identity is securely linked.

**✓ Result:** connection confirmed.

### 5. All set! Account secured

![Privy creates your embedded Solana wallet](/blog/como-comprar-tu-token/step-05.jpg)

Privy automatically creates your embedded Solana wallet. No extensions, no seed
phrase.

**✓ Result:** account & wallet created.

## 02 · Fund your wallet — top up USDC with a card

### 6. You're in, but with no balance

![You see price, circulation, reserve and the curve](/blog/como-comprar-tu-token/step-06.jpg)

You see price, circulation, reserve and the curve. To buy you need USDC: tap
**Fund wallet**.

**→ Your action:** tap «Fund wallet».

### 7. Add funds with a card

![Pay by card and receive USDC instantly](/blog/como-comprar-tu-token/step-07.jpg)

Pay by card and receive USDC instantly. Pick the amount: $30, $10 or $5.

**→ Your action:** pick an amount (e.g. $5).

### 8. Payment approved

![Payment approved · $5 USDC credited](/blog/como-comprar-tu-token/step-08.jpg)

«Payment approved · $5 USDC credited.» Your wallet now holds a USDC balance.

**✓ Result:** wallet funded.

## 03 · Buy your token — pick, confirm, sign

### 9. Choose how many tokens you want

![Type the amount in Buy](/blog/como-comprar-tu-token/step-09.jpg)

Type the amount in **Buy**. The curve sets the price: the earlier you believe,
the lower you pay.

**→ Your action:** tap «Confirm buy».

### 10. Confirm the transaction

![Confirm transaction on Solana, fee under $0.01](/blog/como-comprar-tu-token/step-10.jpg)

«Confirm transaction» opens on **Solana**, with a fee under $0.01. Tap
**Approve**.

**→ Your action:** tap «Approve».

### 11. Transaction signed!

![Transaction signed! The fee was $0.01](/blog/como-comprar-tu-token/step-11.jpg)

«Transaction signed!» The fee was $0.01 and your tokens are in your wallet,
recorded on-chain.

**✓ Result:** purchase complete.

## 04 · Your portfolio — check your holdings

### 12. Check your portfolio

![In Dashboard you see total value, balance and holdings](/blog/como-comprar-tu-token/step-12.jpg)

In **Dashboard** you see total value, balance and your holdings (1 GLI). Send,
top up or launch your token.

**✓ Result:** done! You're now a holder.
